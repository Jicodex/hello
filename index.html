Questions

Before
you apply.

Why is this an application instead of a booking link?
Because we don't take every client. Every engagement is custom-scoped, and we only take on work where we can see a clear path to the outcome. The application lets us understand your goals before either of us commits time.
Who is the ideal BoisePR client?
Ambitious Idaho brands and leaders — whether you're focused on winning your local market or preparing for a national stage. We work best with people who already have something worth amplifying, and who want a real partner in their corner, not a vendor sending invoices.
Do you only work with Boise and Idaho clients?
We're based in Boise and we love working with Idaho brands — but our founder's career spans global work with Disney, Nintendo, Xbox, and more. We can absolutely serve brands beyond the Treasure Valley, though our local market knowledge is a key advantage for Idaho-based companies.
What does an engagement actually cost?
It depends entirely on scope, goals, and timeline. We don't publish fixed retainers because no two engagements are identical. After your application, we'll have a real conversation about what you need — and if we can't justify the investment, we'll tell you.
Are there long-term contracts?
Most engagements are scoped in 90-day cycles so both sides can evaluate and recalibrate. We don't believe in long-term lock-ins. If we're not delivering, you should be able to walk.
How is BoisePR different from hiring a firm from New York or LA?
We bring the same caliber of work — our founder's career proves that — but with the advantage of actually being embedded in the Idaho market. We know the media landscape, the community, the politics, and the pace. An out-of-market firm would bill you to learn all of that. We already know it.
How fast can we get started?
Once your application is reviewed and approved, we'll schedule a strategic alignment session within a few business days. From there, most engagements begin within two weeks.
